This page contains the instructions for adding the main container dependencies in any Kubeinit deployment.

Debian

Depending on your version, run:

  • Debian 11
    • 
sed -i '1s#^#deb [trusted=yes] https://packages.kubeinit.org/debian/bullseye/os/amd64 /\n#' /etc/apt/sources.list
  • Debian 12
    • 
sed -i '1s#^#deb [trusted=yes] https://packages.kubeinit.org/debian/bookworm/os/amd64 /\n#' /etc/apt/sources.list

Ubuntu

Depending on your version, run:

  • Ubuntu 20.04 focal
    • 
sed -i '1s#^#deb [trusted=yes] https://packages.kubeinit.org/ubuntu/focal/os/amd64 /\n#' /etc/apt/sources.list
  • Ubuntu 22.04 Jammy

sed -i '1s#^#deb [trusted=yes] https://packages.kubeinit.org/ubuntu/jammy/os/amd64 /\n#' /etc/apt/sources.list

CentOS Stream

Depending on your version, run:

  • CentOS Stream (8, 9)
    • 
cat << EOF > /etc/yum.repo.d/fedora-kubeinit.repo
[kubeinit]
name=kubeinit
baseurl=https://packages.kubeinit.org/fedora/$releasever/os/$basearch/
enabled=1
gpgcheck=0
sslverify=0
EOF
